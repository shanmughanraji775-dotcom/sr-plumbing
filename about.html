About SR Plumbing & Electrical Service

M. Shanmughan

With over 30 years of extensive experience in both plumbing and electrical services, M. Shanmughan has established SR Plumbing & Electrical Service as a trusted name in Chennai. His unwavering commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction has made him a preferred choice for residential and commercial clients throughout the region.

M. Shanmughan's journey in the industry spans three decades, during which he has honed his skills in all aspects of plumbing and electrical work. From complex installations to emergency repairs, he brings a wealth of knowledge and expertise to every project. His hands-on approach ensures that each job is completed to the highest standards, with meticulous attention to detail and a focus on long-lasting solutions.

What sets SR Plumbing & Electrical Service apart is the perfect combination of exceptional workmanship and affordable pricing. M. Shanmughan believes that quality service should be accessible to everyone, which is why the company motto is "Best work in affordable price." This philosophy has earned the trust of countless customers who appreciate both the superior quality of work and the reasonable rates.

The company specializes in a wide range of services including pipe installations, leak repairs, electrical wiring, fixture installations, circuit repairs, and preventive maintenance. Whether it's a minor repair or a major installation project, M. Shanmughan ensures that every task is handled with professionalism, efficiency, and integrity.

Based in Choolaimedu, Chennai, SR Plumbing & Electrical Service has been serving the local community with dedication and reliability. The company's reputation is built on timely service, transparent pricing, and customer-centric approach. M. Shanmughan's extensive experience, combined with his commitment to staying updated with the latest techniques and technologies, ensures that clients receive modern, efficient, and safe solutions for all their plumbing and electrical needs.

Experience. Excellence. Affordability. That's what you get with SR Plumbing & Electrical Service, where 30 years of expertise meets customer-first service at prices that won't break the bank.
